Our Mission

"We are passionate about presenting the truth of the Bible to children and nothing brings us more joy than to see young people learn scripture and lessons froms God's word. Jewels' Bible Club allows us to do just that. As the children arrive, they are fillled with excitement. While we work hard to ensure the meetings are fun and relevant, they are highlt structured with the focus at all times being on God's Word."

Who Are We?

Jewels' Bible Club is a childrens' club whose goal is to share and teach the word of God in ways they can learn, understand and respond. It is where children come and learn about God's word and love for them.
